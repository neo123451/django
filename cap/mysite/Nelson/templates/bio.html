Biography

After becoming involved in the anti-apartheid movement in his 20s, Mandela joined the African National Congress in 1942. His birth name was Rolihlahla Mandela. "Rolihlahla" in the Xhosa language literally means "pulling the branch of a tree," he went on to become the first in his family to attend school.To the day Nelson Mandela is a idol to manny as well as a inspiration
